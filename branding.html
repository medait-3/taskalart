* Our Process

Every startup has their own unique needs. We work with you to define a plan that helps you reach your goals — whether that includes branding, web-design, creative assets, or all of the above.

Our projects start with developing brands - whether you are new to the field, in the pre-launch stage, or a company looking to rebrand. We have refined our methods over time, which consist of two parts: brand strategy and brand system.

Phase 1: Brand strategy

This foundational phase lays the groundwork for any branding and design decisions moving forward.

Descovery

We’ll learn as much as possible about your company, products, vision, and goals.

Research

We’ll get a deeper understanding of your audience and current trends in your market.

Breif

We’ll distill what we’ve learned in a brief that lays out positioning and messaging opportunities. It will guide our decisions in the next phase as we start bringing together the building blocks of your brand.

Phase 2: Brand system

This is where your brand comes to life through brainstorming, design, execution and documentation.

Naming

We begin here if your startup needs a new name.

Visual Identity

Logo, color palette, Typography and so are the core elements of your visual identity. This phase also defines art direction for creative visual content.

Voice & Tone

We’ll work with you to further shape your brand’s values a personality by establishing voice and tone principles. How you speak to your audience matters as much as how you look.

Brand Guide

Your brand guide serves as a blueprint for all communication moving forward. Some of the items that we document include usage guidelines for the logo and color palette, voice & tone principles, brand values, etc...
